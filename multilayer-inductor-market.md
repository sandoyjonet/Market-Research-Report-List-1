<p><p>The multilayer inductor market consists primarily of two types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT inductors are compact and ideal for automated assembly processes, making them suitable for high-density applications. In contrast, THT inductors are typically larger and are inserted into pre-drilled holes on circuit boards, providing robustness and ease of manual assembly. Both types cater to diverse electronic applications, with a growing demand driven by advancements in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
