<p><p>The thermocouple temperature sensor market consists of two main types: contact and non-contact thermocouple sensors. Contact thermocouples operate by directly touching the material being measured, providing accurate temperature readings and applications in industrial processes. In contrast, non-contact thermocouples measure temperature from a distance, using infrared technology to gauge surface temperatures without physical contact, ideal for hazardous or moving objects. Both types are essential in various sectors, including manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare, to ensure precise temperature monitoring and control.</p></p>
