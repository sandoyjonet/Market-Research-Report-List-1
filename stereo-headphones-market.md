<p><p>The stereo headphones market is categorized into three main types: in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear headphones. In-ear headphones, also known as earphones or earbuds, offer portability and sound isolation, making them popular for active lifestyles. On-ear headphones rest on the ears, providing a balance of sound quality and comfort without the bulk of over-ear models. Over-ear headphones encase the ears entirely, delivering immersive audio experiences and superior noise isolation, appealing to audiophiles and gamers alike. Each type caters to diverse consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
