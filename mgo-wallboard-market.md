<p><p>The MgO wallboard market is categorized into three types based on thickness. Thin wallboards, with a thickness below 8 mm, are lightweight and suitable for applications requiring flexibility and ease of installation. Medium wallboards, between 8-15 mm thick, offer enhanced durability and fire resistance, making them ideal for general construction purposes. Thick wallboards, exceeding 15 mm in thickness, provide superior strength and thermal insulation, often used in more demanding environments such as industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
