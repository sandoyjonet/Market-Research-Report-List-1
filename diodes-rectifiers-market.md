<p><p>The diodes and rectifiers market comprises various types, each serving specific functions. Bridge rectifiers convert AC to DC, vital in power supply applications. PIN diodes, known for their fast switching capabilities, are used in RF and microwave applications. TVS diodes and ESD suppressors protect circuits from voltage spikes and electrostatic discharge. Current regulator diodes maintain a constant current in circuits. The "others" category includes various specialized diodes like zener diodes and Schottky diodes, addressing niche applications in electronics.</p></p>
