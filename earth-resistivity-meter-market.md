<p><p>The Earth Resistivity Meter market is segmented into Single Channel and Multi-Channel types. Single Channel meters measure resistivity at one point at a time, making them suitable for simple surveys and smaller projects. In contrast, Multi-Channel meters can simultaneously measure multiple points, significantly enhancing efficiency and data collection speed for larger scale investigations. This versatility makes Multi-Channel meters ideal for complex geological assessments and extensive site evaluations, while Single Channel meters remain prevalent for more focused applications.</p></p>
